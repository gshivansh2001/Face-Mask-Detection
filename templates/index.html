Real Time Face Mask Detection System

  • In the backend the project utilizes a Mask Detection model trained and saved using tensorflow, keras and OpenCV.It uses a pre-trained MobileNetV2 model for training the model through transfer learning
  • The Trained Model has a Training accuracy of 99.42% and Validation accuracy of 97.45%

Dataset for training the model

The dataset consists of 4,120 images belonging to two categories:

  • with_mask: 2157 images
  • without_mask: 1963 images

How this Mask detection system works?

A colorful rectangular box is generated around the detected faces and at the top of the box is the probability detected by the model that the detected face is wearing a mask. The colour of the mask also changes as:

  • with mask:Green
  • without mask:Red
